# <a name="invoice-schedules-on-project-based-quote-lines"></a><span data-ttu-id="021b4-103">Planificacions de facturació en línies d'oferta basades en projectes</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021b4-103">Invoice schedules on project-based quote lines</span></span>

## <a name="create-a-time-and-material-invoice-schedule-for-a-project-based-quote-line"></a><span data-ttu-id="021b4-111">Crear una planificació de factura de temps i materials per a una oferta basada en projectes</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="021b4-111">Create a Time and material invoice schedule for a project-based quote line</span></span>
